🔒 Kebijakan Privasi

Aplikasi Sensor Gempa – Earthquake Detector Offline tidak mengumpulkan, menyimpan, atau membagikan data pribadi pengguna.

Seluruh proses pendeteksian dilakukan secara lokal (on-device) menggunakan sensor bawaan perangkat, tanpa koneksi internet.

⚙️ Izin yang Digunakan

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — untuk menampilkan peringatan penting, status sensor, atau pembaruan aplikasi.
  • VIBRATE — agar alarm dapat bergetar ketika terdeteksi guncangan.
  • WAKE_LOCK — menjaga perangkat tetap aktif selama alarm berbunyi.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE — memastikan layanan sensor tetap berjalan di latar depan meskipun layar mati.
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM — menjamin alarm jadwal aktif tepat waktu tanpa keterlambatan.
  • REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS — agar sistem tidak menunda layanan sensor.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE_DATA_SYNC (Android 15 ke atas) — digunakan hanya untuk pemrosesan lokal, bukan sinkronisasi jaringan.

💰 Iklan dan Monetisasi

Aplikasi ini menampilkan iklan dari Google AdMob. AdMob dapat menggunakan Advertising ID untuk menampilkan iklan yang lebih relevan.

Tidak ada data pribadi seperti nama, email, atau lokasi yang dikumpulkan oleh pengembang.

Aplikasi dapat berfungsi tanpa koneksi internet (offline).

Nadnad Studio presents Sensor Gempa – Earthquake Detector Offline as a small contribution for public safety.
With Allah’s permission, this app was created to detect vibrations using the phone’s internal sensors and trigger an alarm — serving as an additional early warning during earthquakes, especially while sleeping.
Please ensure the alarm remains active.

This app runs entirely on-device, collects no personal data, and works without an internet connection.
We warmly welcome any feedback for future improvements.

🔒 Privacy Policy

The app does not collect, store, or share any personal data.
All earthquake detection is processed locally using the device’s built-in accelerometer.

⚙️ Permissions Used

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — to show important alerts and sensor status.
  • VIBRATE — enables vibration when a shake is detected.
  • WAKE_LOCK — keeps the device awake while the alarm is active.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE — keeps sensor monitoring active even when the screen is off.
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM — ensures scheduled alarms trigger precisely on time.
  • REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS — prevents the system from delaying the sensor service.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE_DATA_SYNC (Android 15+) — used strictly for local sensor data processing, not for network sync.

💰 Ads and Monetization

This app displays ads via Google AdMob.
AdMob may use the device’s Advertising ID to serve more relevant ads.
The developer does not collect any personal data such as names, emails, or locations.
The app also functions offline.
