Privacy Policy for Sensor Gempa – Offline (Earthquake Detector – Offline)
Developed by Nadnad Studio (Indonesia).

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This application operates entirely offline and does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All earthquake detection and alert processes are performed locally on your device, without any internet connection.

🧩 About the Developer

Nadnad Studio developed this app as an additional tool to help improve public awareness and preparedness for earthquakes. All vibration detection processes occur on the user’s device through built-in motion sensors — no external servers or online data exchange are involved.

⚙️ Permissions Used

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — Display sensor status and alert notifications.
  • VIBRATE — Activate vibration when strong motion is detected.
  • WAKE_LOCK — Keep the device awake while the alarm is active.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE — Ensure continuous monitoring, even when the screen is off.
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM — Guarantee scheduled alarms trigger on time.
  • REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS — Prevent battery saver mode from delaying detection.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE_DATA_SYNC (Android 15+) — Used only for local processing, not for online data synchronization.

💰 Ads & Monetization

This app displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device’s Advertising ID to deliver more relevant ads. The developer does not collect any personal information such as your name, email, or location. All core functions remain fully operational even without an internet connection.
